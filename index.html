Grammar Worksheets

Writing
Your writing should have at least five paragraphs. An introduction, a body (three paragraphs), and a conclusion. Make sure that you define the main idea in the paragraph. The last sentence of each paragraph should be a transition sentence into the next paragraph. Each paragraph should start with a hook sentence (data, a quote, a newspaper article's link) to draw the readers to your writing. Finally, the conclusion should tie up all of the ideas in your writing.
Choose any of these prompts to write.
